## üìä Market Overview & Problem Statement

### The Indian Street Food Market

India's street food industry is estimated to be worth a whopping USD 41 billion, and it's growing rapidly. About 10 million vendors in India make up a thriving street vendor market estimated to be at around INR 8000 crores daily. Despite this massive market size, vendors face significant challenges in procurement and supply chain management.

### Key Market Statistics
- **Market Size**: USD 41 billion (Street Food Industry)
- **Daily Transaction Volume**: INR 8,000 crores
- **Total Vendors**: ~10 million across India
- **Food Service Market**: USD 49.8 Billion in 2024, expected to reach USD 116.8 Billion by 2033
- **Growth Rate**: CAGR of 10.3% during the forecast period 2024 ‚Äì 2033

### Critical Supply Chain Challenges

#### 1. **Raw Material Procurement Issues**
Procurement of adulterated or contaminated raw materials from unauthorised suppliers to cut costs is a significant problem, particularly for raw meat, poultry, and vegetables, which include potential food-borne pathogens.

#### 2. **Supply Chain Inefficiencies**
Challenges such as inflation and supply chain inefficiencies can impact market performance. Due to the lack of effective and efficient supply chain management practices in India, approximately one-third of Agri-fresh products such as fruits are wasted due to poor supply chain management.

#### 3. **Trust and Quality Assurance**
- Lack of verified supplier networks
- Inconsistent quality of raw materials
- Price volatility and lack of transparency
- Limited access to market information

#### 4. **Infrastructure Limitations**
Lack of storage facilities and infrastructure exacerbates the challenges faced by street food vendors in maintaining quality and reducing waste.

## üìà Case Study: Mumbai Street Food Vendor Transformation

### Background
**Raj's Mumbai Chaat** - A traditional street food stall in Bandra West, Mumbai, serving pani puri, bhel puri, and various Indian street snacks to local customers and office workers.

### Pre-Platform Challenges
- **Inconsistent Supplier Quality**: Raw vegetables and ingredients varied in quality day-to-day
- **Price Volatility**: Onion prices increased by 30% within a week without warning
- **Trust Issues**: Multiple instances of adulterated spices from unknown suppliers
- **Cash Flow Problems**: No credit terms, requiring daily cash payments
- **Time Wastage**: 2-3 hours daily spent visiting multiple suppliers
- **Food Safety Concerns**: Occasional customer complaints about freshness

- Registered on the supply chain platform
- Connected with 15 verified suppliers in Mumbai region
- Set up automated price alerts for key ingredients (onions, tomatoes, spices)
